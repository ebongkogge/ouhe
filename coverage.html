Where We Operate

Our Coverage Area

Headquartered in Isokolo-Limbe with active operations in Buea, Kumba, Tiko, Muyuka — and available for large projects across Cameroon.

Service Locations

South West Region & Beyond

📍

Isokolo-Limbe — HEADQUARTERS ★

Our main office and workshop. We serve all of Limbe including Bota, Bonadikombo, New Town, Mile 4, Down Beach, and surrounding areas. All service lines available.

🗺️

Buea — Active Operations

Molyko, Buea Town, Clerks Quarters, Mile 17, Sasse, Great Soppo, and the University of Buea corridor. Regular site visits — no travel surcharge.

🗺️

Kumba

Expanding operations to Kumba. Available for projects across Kumba town and surrounding areas — contact us to discuss your project and mobilisation.

🗺️

Tiko & Muyuka

Both towns are within our operational radius from Limbe. Available for all service lines — contact us for project enquiries.

🚀

Douala & Littoral Region

For large commercial and institutional projects, we are available to mobilise to Douala and the broader Littoral Region. Minimum project size applies — contact us to discuss.
